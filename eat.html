Jerk Chicken Recipe
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 jalapeno chopped fine
  • 1 1/2 pounds cooked chicken, cubed
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 can pineapple pieces
  • 1/2 cup jerk marinade
  • 15 oz can black beans, drained
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
  1. Cook onion and jalapeno in olive oil till browned, about 7 minutes. transfer to bowl.
  2. Place chicken and salt and pepper in skillet. Cook till browned. About 7 minutes.
  3. Add pineapple and marinade, finish cooking chicken until done.
  4. Add beans, cilantro, and onion, jalapenos to chicken. Stir and serve over rice.
